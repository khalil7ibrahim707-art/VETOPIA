About Our Clinic

I am Dr. Mohamed Ayman, the founder of Vetopia Clinic. With years of experience in veterinary medicine, I am dedicated to providing the highest standard of medical and surgical care for pets.

At Vetopia, we believe that every pet deserves to be treated with love and respect. Our clinic is equipped with modern facilities to ensure accurate diagnosis and effective treatment. Whether it's a routine check-up, vaccination, or a complex surgery, your pet is in safe hands.

Our Services

Medical Examinations

Comprehensive health checks to ensure your pet is in top condition. Early detection and treatment of illnesses.

Vaccinations

Essential vaccinations to protect your pets from common and dangerous diseases. Customized vaccination schedules.

Surgery

Professional surgical procedures including spaying, neutering, and soft tissue surgeries in a sterile environment.

Pet Grooming

Professional grooming services to keep your pet looking and feeling their best. Bathing, trimming, and nail clipping.

Dental Care

Dental check-ups and cleaning to prevent gum disease and ensure your pet has a healthy smile.

Emergency Care

Urgent care services for critical situations. We are here to help when your pet needs us the most.
